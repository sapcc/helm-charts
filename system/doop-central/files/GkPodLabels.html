This check validates required labels on Pods.

Why is this a problem?
Each Pod should define at least a alert-tier or alert-service label that corresponds to an alert channel (similar to the tier and service labels on your alert definitions) to enable delivery of generic Kubernetes-level alerts like KubernetesPodRestartingTooMuch to the more specific channels that the service owners observe.

When value of tier label is os then service label is also required and must have a value as per the Alertmanager config.
How to fix?
Add the missing labels, as reported.
